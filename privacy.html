Privacy & Safety

Your privacy is our priority. We're committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

What Data We Collect

  • Body Scan Data: Measurements and body shape parameters captured during the scanning process.
  • Avatar Parameters: Your personalized 3D avatar data and fit preferences.
  • Profile Information: Name, email, height, and basic account details.
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app to improve recommendations.
  • Fit Feedback: Whether items fit correctly to refine our AI models.

Why We Collect This Data

  • Improve Fit Accuracy: Your measurements help us recommend the right size across different brands.
  • Personalize Recommendations: Understanding your style preferences helps us suggest relevant items.
  • Maintain Service Quality: Analytics help us fix issues and improve the user experience.
  • Continuous Learning: Feedback helps our AI become more accurate over time.

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Secure Cloud Storage: Data is stored on secure, GDPR-compliant cloud infrastructure.
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel can access your data, and only when necessary.
  • No Raw Images Stored: We process body scans into measurements only; original photos are immediately deleted.
  • Regular Audits: Our security practices are regularly reviewed and updated.

How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data. You can delete your information at any time.

  • In-App: Go to Settings → Privacy → "Delete My Data" button.
  • Contact Support: Email us at privacy@wmattire.com with your request.
  • Timeline: All avatar and measurement data will be removed from active systems within 30 days.
  • Download Your Data: Request a copy of all your data before deletion if desired.

WM Attire Does Not Sell Biometric Data. Ever.

Your body measurements, scan data, and avatar information are never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than providing you with accurate fit recommendations. This is our promise to you.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for specific functions:

  • 3D Scanning SDK: Processes body scans on-device when possible.
  • Cloud Storage: Secure AWS/GCP infrastructure for data storage.
  • Payment Processing: Stripe for secure subscription payments (Premium plan).
  • Analytics: Privacy-focused analytics to understand app usage patterns.

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and not use it for their own purposes.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have rights including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to opt out of data processing (where applicable)

Contact privacy@wmattire.com to exercise any of these rights.

Children's Privacy

WM Attire is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please reach out.
